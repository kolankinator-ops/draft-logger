Tier 1 League Filter

Matches league names against these patterns (case-insensitive substring match) — seeded with the current 2026 Tier 1 organizers (PGL Wallachia, ESL One, BLAST Slam, DreamLeague's main seasons, The International, Esports World Cup, Esports Nations Cup) plus their qualifiers. Edit freely, one pattern per line, then preview and confirm before this becomes the actual filter.

New Series Detector

Catches a new tournament series before you'd know to name it yourself: pulls OpenDota's Elo-style team ratings (its equivalent of a power ranking), then checks which recent leagues outside your saved Tier 1 list have a high concentration of those top-rated teams playing in them.

Run Historical Backfill

Uses your saved Tier 1 league list above if you've confirmed one; otherwise falls back to leagues.tier IN ('premium','professional') as a rough default. Pulls match-level drafts in descending match_id order until the lookback window is covered. Won't overwrite matches already captured live.

Resolve Team Names

Backfilled matches often carry team IDs without names. This resolves them via OpenDota's team endpoint, paced to stay under rate limits — can take a while for a full year of teams.

Backfill Lane Data

Pulls player_slot, hero_id, lane, lane_role, and is_roaming for every match already in your store (skips matches that already have it). Only these lightweight columns — not the full per-player stat blob, which would reintroduce the storage problem. Needed for the Matchups tab.
